Problem Definition

The first step of this project entailed fine tuning a problem statement. We knew that we wanted to explore Story Maps since our collab client, Friends of Temagami, requested a Story Map diving into the history of their organization. Delving into the technology now would prepare us for collaborating with them later. Additionally, since neither of us were familiar with the Friends of Temagami or the Temagami region in general, this project offered a great opportunity to begin learning more about the organization and area.

Initially, we were going to focus our Story Map on the Friends of Temagami organization, giving us a focus in line with our collab project expectations that we would be able to build upon later. However, once Shawn recommended making a resource that would be something us, as people who have never visited Temagami, would like to see, it was immediately obvious that this would make for a great topic. Not only would this project form a great introduction to the area for us, it would ensure that our collab partner that was not enrolled in geom99 with us wouldn't be left out of completing collab tasks.
